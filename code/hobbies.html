Take a look at my Hobbies!

My hobbies and pasttimes while not a lot, I really enjoy them to the fullest, and finding other related activities are very fulfilling! Please take some time to look and explore some of my activties...

Video Games

One of my favorite pastimes is to play video games like any other kid my age. I really enjoy all types of games, but there are some genres that hold a special place in my heart such as Nintendo games. Each time I play a game from Nintendo, I am instantly reminded of better times, and I can find my self spending hours upon hours playing levels in Mario and exploring vast worlds of the Legend of Zelda. Each Nintendo sound track has a special sense of timelessness, and Nintendo will forever go down as my favorite game studio.

Movies

Around the time I started playing video games, I was introduced into the forever expanding world of Star Wars, at around 5-6 years old. Since then, I have continued to be a huge fan of the series, to the point where I can rattle off any fact from the top of my head to 10 minutes of a movie, all the way to lore facts so deep it would take a normal person years to find out about. I love this series to its very core, and will never let it go.

Other hobbies of mine

Outside of these regular activities, for the past 4 years, I have been heavily associated with the non profit organization FIRST, specifically their First Tech Challenge. Currently I am on team 12611 TechNova, two time Washington State Inspire Award winners and 2018 World Champions. I work primarily on build and 3D design for our team.
